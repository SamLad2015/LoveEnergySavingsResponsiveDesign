Switching business water supplier FAQs
Can I switch my business water supplier? +-

In April 2017, water deregulation was introduced in England which allowed over 1.2 million businesses, charities and public sector organisations to switch their water supplier.

This means that if your premises are primarily used for business purposes, you can shop around and switch water supplier.
